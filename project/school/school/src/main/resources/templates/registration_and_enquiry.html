     Admission Details

         When considering all applicants, we take into account both the suitability of our educational programme for the prospective student and the availability of places within the school.

The earliest acceptance of application material is three months prior to planned attendance and/or January of that particular year.

In recognition of our mission as an International School, it is possible that, as class sizes approach the maximum, remaining places may be reserved for expatriate international students.

     Our Vision

MBIS is Child Centric-‘Children Matter Most’ -not only as recipients but as ‘partners and contributors of knowledge production, insights, innovations and perspectives’!

There is much to be learnt from them rather than teaching and instructing them all the time with an adult supremacy. We do not encourage the use of adult frame and highhandedness in dealing with them and their affairs. The schools, therefore, must offer an environment where they have the freedom to express, evolve and experiment. They have to be treated as equals and not subservient. For us the school education offers foundation for life and therefore, it must excite, encourage and prepare the children for meaningful pursuits according to their orientation and choice. MBIS’s vision resonates the above.

     Supporting document required with application

 

The admission procedure is simple and straightforward. Kindly follow the steps below :

1. The Registration form should be filled online or submit  to the School Office between 9 AM to 2 PM, Monday to Saturday.

2. An incomplete or illegible Application Form, without a photograph and other documents, will not be accepted.

3. List of the documents to be attached: -

  •  Birth Certificate copy .(original for verification at the time of admission)
  •  Transfer Certificate Copy
  •  5 Passport Size Photographs of child.
  •  2 Passport Size Photographs of each parent.
  •  1 group photograph with child.
  •  ID & Address proof copy of parents.
  •  Aadhar card copy of Parent and child.
  •  Previous year report card copy. (For Class II onwards).
  •  Parent’s Transfer Certificate (For mid-session admission)
  •  Vaccination Card copy (Upto Grade- II).
  •  Blood Group report.

Note: - Original documents are required to be brought for verification at the time of admission.

Parents are requested to fill in the Application Form on line here or downloaded from our school website.
