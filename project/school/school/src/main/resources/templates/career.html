Career

Manava Bharati International School, Patna requires the following staff urgently

Candidates seeking employment should have experience in a reputed Public School, excellent communication skills, excellent academic record, requisite experience, proven ability in Curricular and Co-Curricular activities and adequate computer literacy. Salary and allowances as per the School norms. ' Our vision is building a future ready citizen by offering school education for holistic development of the children focused around their creative abilities, self-efficacy and psychological and emotional well being '.

We believe in ‘ Child centric perspective, collaborative and inclusive approach ’ where every body matters and everybody has the ability and intention to contribute in the conviction

In our scheme of things, we follow a bottom up structure and deliberative and consultative process. This is likely to encourage spontaneous ideation and innovation, experimentation and scope for changes and modification. Under our structure there would be ample scope for everybody to ideate, initiate, debate and question in the process of knowledge production and perspective building. The social and institutional position, academic qualification, implicit and explicit flamboyancy is not encouraged to take over and influence appropriate ideas, vision and strategy.
